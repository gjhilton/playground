Other Recreations in Whitby

Whitby is famous for its Dracula connections, and the town celebrates this heritage with a variety of events and recreations throughout the year.

Annual Events

  • Whitby Goth Weekend: A twice-yearly festival celebrating gothic culture, music, and fashion, drawing visitors from around the world.
  • Dracula Society Gatherings: Fans of the novel and film adaptations meet in Whitby for readings, performances, and tours.
  • Costumed Walks: Guided tours and walks in costume, exploring the sites from the novel and Whitby's history.

Film & TV

Whitby has featured in numerous Dracula adaptations, documentaries, and gothic productions, making it a popular filming location.
