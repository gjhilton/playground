Getting Started:

  • Choose Promenade for an actual walking tour of Whitby
  • Choose Parlour to experience the content from anywhere

Navigation:

  • Swipe right to go back to previous screens
  • Tap locations on the map to view content
  • Use the audio toggle button to control narration

Promenade Mode:

  • Requires location services to be enabled
  • Follow the walking directions to each location
  • Tap "I'm at this location" when you arrive
  • Content will auto-play with audio narration

Parlour Mode:

  • No location services required
  • Tap any location to view content immediately
  • Perfect for experiencing the tour from home

Content Features:

  • Audio narration synchronized with text
  • Historical information about each location
  • Excerpts from Bram Stoker's Dracula
  • Progress tracking for visited locations

Extras:

  • Read the full novel chapter by chapter
  • Learn about other Dracula adaptations
  • View credits and acknowledgments

Tips:

  • Keep your device charged during the tour
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Respect the historical locations
